About the Designer

Hello, my name is Gretthel Luna. I consider myself a jack of all trades in the Digital World. I have over 10 years of experience in Digital Marketing. As I got deeper into the Digital Marketing world I realized the need to learn to code and read code.

In 2019, I received my Associates in Programming and Analysis. This has allowed me to understand how websites and other apps work and apply this knowledge to the websites and landing pages that I use when managing my client's Online Marketing campaings. I have also seen the need to create new Apps for Digital Marketing companies that can help them manage campaigns at a large scale. The Associates Degree has only taken so far though and I continue to expand my knowledge in coding.

I don't just have a degree in Programming but also a Bachelor's in Architecture. Although, I never went into the field of Architecture I am able to apply my design knowledge towards creating programs and websites.
